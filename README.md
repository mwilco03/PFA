# USAF PFA Readiness Tracker

**Track your fitness. Project your readiness. Pass your PFA.**

A mobile-first web app for USAF Airmen to check fitness performance against 2026 PFA scoring standards, project future readiness, and generate supervisor reports. Works on your phone at the gym or track. No login, no signup, all your data stays on your device.

## What This Does

- **Instant Score Estimates** - Enter your workout data, see your composite score immediately
- **Track Progress** - Save multiple self-checks, visualize trends over time
- **Project Readiness** - See where you'll be on your next PFA date based on current trajectory
- **Generate Reports** - Create formatted reports to share with your supervisor or UFPM
- **100% Private** - All data stays in your browser. Nothing sent to a server. Ever.

### Supports All 2026 PFA Exercises

- **Cardio**: 2-mile run, HAMR shuttle run, 2km walk (profile)
- **Strength**: Push-ups, Hand-Release Push-Ups (HRPU)
- **Core**: Sit-ups, Cross-Leg Reverse Crunches (CLRC), Forearm Plank
- **Body Composition**: Waist-to-Height Ratio (WHtR)
- **Medical Profiles**: Component exemptions supported (AF Form 469)
- **Altitude**: Corrections for 7 high-altitude USAF bases (CO/WY/NM)

## How to Use It

### First Time: Create Your Profile

1. Open the app ‚Üí **Profile** tab
2. Enter your date of birth and select gender
3. Tap **Copy D-Code** and save it somewhere (Notes app works great)

Your D-code is ~9 characters like `D1-abc123ef`. It contains only your DOB and gender. **No name, rank, or unit.**

### Record a Self-Check

1. **Self-Check** tab ‚Üí enter today's date
2. Pick your exercises and enter your performance
3. See your score estimate update live
4. Tap **Copy S-Code** to save this workout

Your S-code is ~22 characters like `S1-xyz789abcdef`. It contains only your performance data. **No personal info.**

### Track Your Progress

1. **History** tab ‚Üí paste your S-codes
2. See trends, flag outliers, track improvement

### Project Future Readiness

1. **Project** tab ‚Üí enter your next PFA date
2. See projected scores and gaps for each component
3. Get weekly improvement targets

### Share with Your Supervisor

1. **Report** tab ‚Üí enter rank/name/unit (only for the report, not saved)
2. Copy or print formatted report
3. Share via email/Teams

## Your Data is Private

- **Zero backend** - This is a static website, there's no server collecting data
- **No accounts** - No login, no signup, no password
- **No tracking** - No analytics, no cookies, no third parties
- **Local only** - Everything stays in your browser
- **Your codes** - D-codes and S-codes are YOUR data. Share them or keep them private.

Codes can be pasted into the app on any device to load your data. Lose your codes = lose your data.

## Diagnostic vs Scored Period

- **Mar 1 - Jun 30, 2026**: Diagnostic (non-scored)
- **Jul 1, 2026+**: Official scored PFAs begin

The app auto-detects diagnostic period based on your self-check date.
